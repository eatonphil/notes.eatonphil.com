Welcome!

Hey! I'm Phil. In the past 10 years I've been a developer or manager or founder, at startups and Oracle. I'm currently on the job market! I don't have a CS degree and I'd be happy to chat except for at places that ask me to implement easily-Googleable algorithms in an interview. :)

I quit my job at Oracle in 2021 to start Multiprocess Labs (just me) where I build open-source data tools like DataStation (2.3k+ stars), an open-source data IDE for developers, and dsq (2.5k+ stars), a commandline tool for running SQL queries against JSON, CSV, Parquet, Excel and more.

You can read about my experience doing this for a year here.

Hang out

I host a hacker Discord (~900 members) and a monthly virtual Meetup, Hacker Nights (300+ members), that has featured talks by the founder of bit.io, the creator of rqlite, the author of Napkin Math and many hobbyists hacking on cool tech for fun. Both the Meetup and the Discord are designed to expose devs (and myself) to the internals of databases, compilers, operating systems, browsers, emulators, etc. And to provide a community around this kind of exploration. You are very welcome to join either!

Elsewhere

You can find me on Github, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Goodreads.
